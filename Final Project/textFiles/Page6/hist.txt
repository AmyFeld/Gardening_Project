
		- Arden is a municipality founded in 1900. 

		- It has had direct democracy, elected committees manage the village. 

		- The housing is clustered, 50% dedicated green space with 50 acres of protected forest surrounds the village. 

		- The policy not to cut trees for you must get a permit. Forest Committee manages the woodlands and has tree risk assessments. 

		- There is invasive plant removal as well as path improvements, erosion control, and boundary protection.
