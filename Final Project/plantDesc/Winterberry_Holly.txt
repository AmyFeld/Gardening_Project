Name: Winterberry Holly
Scientific Name: Ilex verticillata
Type: Tree
Size: 6 to 12 feet
Bloom Time: April to July
Fruit: Red and Orange Berries
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Winterberry Holly is a tree that lasts with its bright red berries for the holiday season. White the Winterberry Holly bloom with white, yellow, green and brown flowers, our greenery only has Winterberry Holly with white bloom. While being a beautiful and decorative plant, the berries are poisonous and considered dangerous to any child's curious eyes. Its beauty comes with a bit of a price as it need stender love, care and water.
