Name: Golden Alexanders
Scientific Name: Thaspium trifoliatum
Type: Herb
Size: 3 feet
Bloom Time: April to June
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: Yellow

Synopsis:
Teh Golden Alexander is a herb that is known for its long lasting bloom
time all spring and adding a prairie like feel to any garden. This plant
fills a gap in bloom times where eithe plants have just finished blooming
or are just about to start blooming so that it will help maintain the color
of your garden. Friendly to pollenators, the Golden ALexander is known for
its small yellow flowers whihc have a soft but nice fragrance. Only needing
a medium amount of water, this plant is a simple but well worth addition
to your edge.
