Name: Flat Leaf Parsley
Scientific Name: Petroselinum neapolitanum
Type: Herb
Size: 1 foot
Bloom Time: June to August
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
Flat Leaf Parsley is an herb that loves the summertime! Thriving in the warmer months, the small white flowers that sport
the same fragrance as the rest of the plant. It is known to be hardier than other types of parsley due to it being able
to bloom and survive as the temperature rises, while others tend to whiter and die when it gets too hot. Its primary
growing season is during teh summer so whenever the leaves are ready, you cna pick them and use them in your next dish!
