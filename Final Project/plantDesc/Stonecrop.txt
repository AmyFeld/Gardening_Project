Name: Stonecrop
Scientific Name: Sedum spurium
Type: Herb
Size: 0.33 feet
Bloom Time: September to November
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: Red

Synopsis:
The Stonecrop is a herb that blooms with a variety of colors, including pink flowers,
red flowers, white flowers, etc. Our greenery only offers Stonecrop with a red bloom.
Another frontliner for a garden, the bright red color of its bloom will make it stand
out against the call colors. while blooming late, not make plants bloom during the fall
so it is able to fill that last time slot before the winter comes creeping in. The flowers
have a distinct star shape and are seen as very delicate since they are so small. It
doesn't need much maintenance and low amounts of water, making it ideal for beginners to use.
