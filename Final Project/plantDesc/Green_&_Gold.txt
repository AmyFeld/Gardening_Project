Name: Green & Gold
Scientific Name: Chrysogonum virginianum
Type: Herb
Size: 0 to 1 foot
Bloom Time: April to August
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: Yellow

Synopsis:
The Green And Gold are a fast growing and beginner level plant. Needing only low levels
of water and growing at a rapid pace, they make an excellent decoration to line a garden
or more easily transition a forest edge. Their yellow, star shaped flowers also make sure
to stick around from mid Spring to late Summer, leaving your garden shining as brightly
as the June sun! The flowers also tend to attract butterflies, so there will be wuite a few
flying around when the Green and Gold are in bloom.
