Name: African Blue Basil
Scientific Name: Ocimum kilimandscharicum
Type: Herb
Size: 2 feet
Bloom Time: March to September
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: Purple

Synopsis:
The African Blue Basil lives up to its name for its very distinctive blueish purpleish hue.
Interestingly enough, this plant never produces seeds due it it being a hybrid between 2
different basils. This hybridization also affects its bloom as it consistenly blooms through
almost the entire year. The lavendar colored blooms add a different set of colors to your
garden and make sure your garden maintains some color through every bloom season!
