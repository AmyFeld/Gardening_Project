Name: Red-Twig Dogwood
Scientific Name: Cornus sericea
Type: Shrub
Size: 6 to 12 feet
Bloom Time: May to June
Fruit: Green Berries
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Red-Twig Dogwood is very similar to teh other Dogwood tree available in our greenery. Like the Dogwood, it produces a light colored flower during bloom time but the Red-Twig blooms right as the Dogwood stops. It also provides heavily for wild birds and its leaves in the AUtumn are very colorful. During the winter, the branch don a red hue and stand out greatly through the northeastern winters and snow fall.
