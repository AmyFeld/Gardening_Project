Name: Serviceberry
Scientific Name: Amelanchier laevis
Type: Tree
Size: 12 to 36 feet
Bloom Time: All Year
Fruit: Black, Purple and  Red Berries
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Serviceberry tree is one that will shine through with its white bloom all year round. Its
primarily role is taking care of local animals with its rich berries. The berries it produces
are considered rich in iron and copper and are typically dried like raisins or as part of a cake.
The flowers also produce a wonderful fragrance and the Servicebrry also attracts birds. It only
takes a moderate amount of water but unfortunately are susceptible to many diseases.
