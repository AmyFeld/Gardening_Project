Name: White Oak
Scientific Name: Quercus Alba
Type: Tree
Size: Over 100 feet
Bloom Time: March to May
Fruit: Nuts
Bloom Color: Green

Synopsis:
The White Oak is a strong tree that provides from planting to chopping. White Oak grow
incredibly tall but will guarantee the best shade when the sun is just too much. Its
interesting grey and white bark makes it stand out from other trees and its wood is known
to be one of the most essential lumber in the industry. It takes its time growing in your
yard but only takes moderate amounts of water but the White Oak is sure to last you a lifetime.
