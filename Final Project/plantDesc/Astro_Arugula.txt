Name: Astro Arugula
Scientific Name: Eruca sativa
Type: Herb
Size: 8 to 10 inches
Bloom Time: August to October
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Astro Arugula is an herb that most know from seeing it at groecry stores, being sold
for consumption. This plant, when in season, is best to be harvested in the late summer
to fall and has a distinct nutty flavor, perfect for salads! This herb needs a moderate
amount of water and may sprout small white flowers during harvest which also have a
similar taste and are used as decorations for other foods!
