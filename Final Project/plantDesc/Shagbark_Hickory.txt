Name: Shagbark Hickory
Scientific Name: Cornus florida
Type: Tree
Size: 10 to 25 feet
Bloom Time: March to May
Fruit: Red Berries
Bloom Color: Pink

Synopsis:
The Shagbark Hickory is a tree that grows tall and has a crown of leaves up top. As the
leaves start off as a yellow-green, the transition to Fall sees also the transition into
a regal gold color, perfect for the season. Its hickory nuts are edible and are normally
eaten during the colder months. It also produces milk which is an essential ingredient
for recipes such as corn cakes and hominy.
