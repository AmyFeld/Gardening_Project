Name: Hummingbird Summersweet
Scientific Name: Clethra alnifolia
Type: Shrub
Size: 3 to 6 feet
Bloom Time: July to August
Fruit: Dark Brown Capsules
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Hummingbird Summersweet is a shrub that is compact, gorgeous and slow spreading. It attracts many pollenators
and hummingbirds, as one may have guessed from its name. The flowers will make sure to last during the summer,
givin your garden an added splash of white while also providing nectar during a season where most other plants
may no longer be in bloom. It has excellent resistance to insects and diseases while needing an average amount of
water.
