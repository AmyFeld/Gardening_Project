Name: Sweet Spire
Scientific Name: Itea Virginica
Type: Shrub
Size: 3 to 6 feet
Bloom Time: April to June
Fruit: Brown Fruit
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Sweet Spire is a show stopping plant for any garden. It's showy nature comes from it's plants and foliage colors as it, too, matures into a beautiful Autumn red. It's white flowers provide for nectar based insects and attracts birds as well, providing the native fauna a wonderful habitat. Be careful, however, as it's beauty deos come with a price of high water usage and maintianance.
