Name: Milkweed
Scientific Name: Nyssa aquatica
Type: Herb
Size: 2.5 to 3 feet
Bloom Time: May to August
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: Purple

Synopsis:
The Milkweed is a plant that most know for its use to the monarch butterfly. Monarch caterpillars are commmonly eating away at milkweed for nutrition which is vital for their growth into beautiful monarchs. Milkweed can bloom with green, pink or purple flowers but our greenery only offers Milkweed with a purple bloom. The milk part of the name comes from its sap, which feels and looks milky. Their stems are also found to have a greenish purple hue.
