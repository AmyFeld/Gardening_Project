Name: Thundercloud Plum
Scientific Name: Prunus cerasifera
Type: Tree
Size: 15 to 20 feet
Bloom Time: April
Fruit: Purple Fruit
Bloom Color: Pink

Synopsis:
The Thundercloud Plum is a tree that has leaves that stand out amongst green foliage for being a dark red
color. During early to mid spring, the branches of the Thundercloud Plum bloom with small pink blossoms
while towards the summer, the flowers fade to show small plums ready to be picked and eaten! The flowers
it has give off a gentle fragrance and birds will be able to enjoy the small plums as well. It may be
slightly spiny so be wary. It maintains its burghendy color throughout all warmer seasons as well.
