Name: Girard's Pleasant White Azalea
Scientific Name: Bigelowia nudata
Type: Shrub
Size: 2 feet
Bloom Time: May
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: White

Synopsis:
The Girard's Pleasant White Azalea is a star when it comes to its bloom. Its flowers have an elegant but delicate
shape as May rolls around and are one of the most showy flowers in our greenery! Its adds an air of refinement with
gentle white blooms and unique flower shape. This shrub needs a moderate amount of water and its flowers provide
for the humminbird and butterfly population with its sweet nectar. Being an evergreen plant, the Girard's Pleasant
White Azalea alos can be used for its distinct leaf type as the fall turns to winter.
