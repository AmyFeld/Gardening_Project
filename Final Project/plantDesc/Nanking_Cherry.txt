Name: Nanking Cherry
Scientific Name: Prunus tomentosa
Type: Tree
Size: 8 feet
Bloom Time: March to April
Fruit: Red Berries
Bloom Color: Pink

Synopsis:
The Nanking Cherry is a tree that sprouts small pinkish buds during the early spring and will produces
small, grape sized fruit that are its major appeal. These red fruit are said to have a sweet but tart
flavor that can be eaten fresh or made into pies or jellies. The bark of the tree develop a lustrous
orange color and exfoialtes vertically with age. It is a resiliant plant to hot summers and drought but
make sure to still water it regularly.
