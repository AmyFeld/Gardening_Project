Name: Okame Cherry Tree
Scientific Name: Prunus 'Okame'
Type: Tree
Size: 15 to 30 feet
Bloom Time: March to April
Fruit: None
Bloom Color: Pink

Synopsis:
The Okame Cherry Tree is a tree that you would see if you've ever gone to the Washington DC National
Cherry Blossom Festival. These gorgeous trees are a fleeting beauty amongst all, their delicate pink
flowers havea a soft fragrance and only last for a short while during the early spring. In the fall,
these trees don the mroe warm colors of orange and red. It can adjust to most soils but needs a fair
amount of water to thrive. The Okame Cherry Tree is sure to take the spotlight in your garden!
